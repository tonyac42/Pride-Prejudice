Pride & Prejudice

THE GREATEST ROMANCE EVER PERPETRATED

Characters

Mr. Fitwilliam Darcy - The handsomest and richest man who also has the most honesty, courage and the finest character. But can he show it?

Miss Elizabeth Bennet - Pretty and wise beyond her years, she never bows to social pressure. But can she admit when she's wrong?

Mr. Whickham - An officer and (almost) a gentleman with engaging manners. But is there something behind the eyes?

Miss Lydia Bennet - The silliest girl in all of England and probably beyond. But will she ever be a married woman?

Mr. Bingley - Handsome and rich and charming and he must have you dance. But will he make designs upon what he desires?

Miss Jane Bennet - The handsomest creature you have ever seen. Unlimited goodness. But will anyone make designs upon her?

Miss Caroline Bingley - CRINGE
